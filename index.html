Welcome to the Galactic Garden, a captivating journey through the universe's exoplanets, each depicted as a unique flower. Here's how to interpret this cosmic flora:

  • Petal Size: The larger the petal, the greater the planet's radius, showcasing its size.
  • Petal Shape: Each petal's curved path gives the flower its distinct appearance, mirroring the unique nature of each planet.
  • Stem Length: Represents the distance of the planet from Earth. A longer stem means the planet is farther away.
  • Position in the Garden: Indicates the year of the planet's discovery. Flowers positioned to the right represent more recent discoveries.
  • Color Coding: Each color signifies a different type of planet: red for Gas Giants, grey for Terrestrial planets, blue for Neptune-like, and green for Super Earths. This helps in quickly identifying the planet type.
Explore this interactive garden by selecting different planet types from the filter, and click on any flower to discover more about that exoplanet. Simply click anywhere outside the details panel to close it.
